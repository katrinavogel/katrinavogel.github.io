Sable’s Journey




Many years ago when I was younger, I wrote a short story about a lonely raven
from a magical land who sought to discover who he was. After attempting to
begin an intense graphic novel set in the same world, I became so overwhelmed
with designing and drawing all the characters and settings that I had to put
that aside and start simple. That’s when I rediscovered this short story and
realized it was the perfect start, not just for me as an artist, but for
inviting others into my imagination. I’m currently sketching out all pages of
the comic as time permits, but am hoping to begin sharing sometime this summer.

In the meantime, meet Sable.
